Brainsharer Multi-user annotation modalities

Mode Description Feature Benefit
Individual Anatomist creates/edits annotations at own pace but may share afterward Create individual annotations, not time-synchronized Annotation capability, dissemination to others (publications, reference)
Team Anatomists create/edit annotations [different structures or different sections], store on platform and are aggregated for QC review Aggregated individual annotations or "divide and conquer", not time- synchronized Productivity Increase
Instructor Single user has create/edit capability but many observers may view annotation creation live One-to-many modality, time- synchronized Training tool
Collaboration Full create/edit capability by >1 anatomist (Note: May be combined with "view only" observers) Many-to-many modality, time- synchronized Full collaboration
